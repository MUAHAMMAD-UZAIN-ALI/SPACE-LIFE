My Astronomy Equipment

A look at the gear I use to capture the wonders of the night sky.

My Current Setup

Main Telescope

A Celestron EdgeHD 8" with a Hyperstar 4.5. This apochromatic refractor is my workhorse for deep-sky imaging. It provides exceptional color correction and sharp stars across the entire field of view.

Astrophotography Camera

ZWO ASI294MC Pro. A powerful cooled camera with a sensitive sensor, perfect for capturing faint nebulae and galaxies in long exposures without thermal noise.

Equatorial Mount

Sky-Watcher EQ6-R Pro. This heavy-duty computerized mount is essential for tracking celestial objects accurately during long-exposure astrophotography sessions.
